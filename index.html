ABOUT THE GAME

A fast-paced food truck cooking adventure

In TruckFest, you'll be juggling three food trucks at once while festival-goers are literally breathing down your neck for their orders - it's stressful but in a good way. The gameplay combines time management, upgrade systems, and visually satisfying cooking actions. As players progress, they unlock new trucks, recipes, and increasingly challenging festival stages. Great for quick breaks when you want something that actually gets your adrenaline going. These games don't mess around.

HOW TO PLAY

Cook, serve, and upgrade your food trucks

  • Prepare Festival Food

    Cook burgers, tacos, drinks, and street snacks.

  • Serve Customers Quickly

    Keep up with fast-moving festival crowds.

  • Upgrade Your Trucks

    Improve equipment for faster cooking and service.

  • Unlock New Festival Locations

    Travel through different food truck events.

  • Master Time Management

    Balance speed, accuracy, and customer satisfaction.
